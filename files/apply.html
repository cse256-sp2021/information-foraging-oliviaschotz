Application Procedure

Step 1: Visit

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

Step 2: Fill out Application

You may fill out an Application for Admission online. You may also print out and return the pdf version:
Online form: Online Application for Admission WUNS
Print-out pdf: Print-out Application for Admission WUNS

Step 3: Pay Application Fee

An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school. After this is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list.
